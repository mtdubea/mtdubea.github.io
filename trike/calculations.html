📝 Sample Calculations & Analysis

1. Mass, Weight & Velocity

Total weight: W = m × g = 209.4 lbm × 32.174 ft s⁻² = 209.4 lbf ≈ 932 N (95 kg).

Average lap speed: vavg = L ⁄ t = 3 485 ft ⁄ 211 s ≈ 16.5 ft s⁻¹ = 11.3 mph.

Wheel revs per lap (20 in front wheel): Nrot = L ⁄ C ≈ 665 rev.

2. Energy & Power Budget

Kinetic energy @ 11.3 mph: KE ≈ 886 ft lbf ≈ 1.20 kJ.

Work per lap (84 W rider): Wmech = P t ≈ 17.7 kJ.

3. Stability & Cornering

θtip = atan((19.5 in⁄2) ⁄ 23 in) ≈ 23° → ay ≈ 0.42 g.

Max safe speed (R = 15 ft): vmax ≈ 9.8 mph.

4. Cadence vs Speed

v (mph) ≈ 0.357 × Nrpm → 11.3 mph ≈ 32 rpm, 14.5 mph ≈ 41 rpm.

5. Strength & Deflection Checks
  • Axle (4140 HT): τ = 903 psi, σb = 39.7 ksi → σvm ≈ 39.7 ksi, FoS ≈ 1.7.
  • Seat-rail deflection: δ = 0.062 in < 0.25 in spec.
  • Frame FEA: σvm,max = 2.795 × 10⁸ N m⁻², σy = 6.204 × 10⁸ N m⁻² → FoS ≈ 2.22.
