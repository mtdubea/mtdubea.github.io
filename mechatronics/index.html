🚁 Mission Snapshot

Drone‑suspended payload that detects obstacles, water contact, temperature excursions, and releases secondary samplers on command. Powered by dual rails: 6×AA pack for logic + sensors, plus a hot‑swappable 9 V battery for high‑current actuators.

  • MPU‑9250 IMU
  • HC‑SR04 Ultrasonic
  • Water Probe
  • NTC Thermistor
  • SG90 Servo
  • 16×2 I²C LCD

🛠 Core Firmware Modules

SketchDescription
master_payload.ino Main loop—coordinates all mission tasks & state transitions.
