⚡ Mission Snapshot

Drone-suspended payload that detects obstacles, water contact, temperature excursions, and automatically releases secondary samplers. Powered by dual rails: 6×AA pack for logic & sensors, plus a hot-swappable 9 V battery for high-current actuators (spare 9 V carried for redundancy).

  • MPU9250 IMU
  • HC-SR04 Ultrasonic
  • Water Probe
  • NTC Thermistor
  • SG90 Servo
  • 16×2 I²C LCD

Fully-wired payload ready for field test.
