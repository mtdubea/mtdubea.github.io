This drone-suspended payload uses the following components:

  • MPU9250 IMU: Tracks motion, tilt, and heading (like a phone's internal gyroscope).
  • HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor: Measures distance to nearby objects using sound pulses.
  • Water Probe: Detects contact with water to trigger sample release.
  • NTC Thermistor: Detects temperature changes, including sudden heat exposure.
  • SG90 Servo: Physically releases mounted payloads during field operation.
  • 16√ó2 I¬≤C LCD: Displays real-time readings for field debugging and monitoring.
