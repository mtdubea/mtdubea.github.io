🖥️ I²C LCD Support

Local copy of LiquidCrystal_I2C is included to allow full offline builds. No external library installation is required.

  • LiquidCrystal_I2C.h / .cpp — I²C LCD driver
  • keywords.txt — IDE keyword mappings

🔋 Power Architecture

Logic & sensors: 6×AA → 5 V via regulator
High-current actuators: Dedicated 9 V
Backup: Redundant 9 V mounted for fast swap during testing
