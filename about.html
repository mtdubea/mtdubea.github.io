About Me

I’m Matthew Dubea, a Mechanical Engineering graduate from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, driven by a passion for precision, process improvement, and technical design. My work spans CAD/FEA, hands-on manufacturing, QA/QC, Lean/Six Sigma, and real-world field installation. With experience in HVAC, chemical plant work, and national competition-winning fabrication, I bridge practical know-how with analytical depth.

I believe great design lives at the intersection of simplicity and performance. Whether welding steel for a race trike, debugging automation code, or reverse engineering better workflows with Python, I’m always chasing smarter solutions. I’m currently pursuing my FE/PE licensure and continually building out my professional toolkit.
