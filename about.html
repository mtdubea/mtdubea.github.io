About Me

Designing How Things Are Made

I’m a mechanical engineer who loves the factory floor as much as CAD. My sweet spot is manufacturing and process optimization—designing parts and the tooling/fixtures that build them, then tightening the flow with data, automation, and disciplined quality. I blend hands-on build experience with FEA, GD&T, and Six Sigma thinking to deliver throughput, reliability, and cost control.

Where I’m headed next: a design/manufacturing team that owns DFM/DFA, jigs & fixtures, line balance, and continuous improvement. I enjoy simplifying processes, turning repeat work into scripts, and building dashboards that keep production honest. Tools I reach for: SOLIDWORKS, AutoCAD, Siemens NX, Python, Power BI, and SAP.
