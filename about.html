🔧 Engineering Background

I’m a Mechanical Engineering graduate from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, with a strong foundation in CAD/FEA, process design, and hands-on fabrication. I’ve built race trikes, autonomous robots, and multi-sensor payloads using SolidWorks, Python, MATLAB, and microcontrollers.

My portfolio focuses on functional design, efficiency, and innovation. I’ve led fabrication using TIG welding, CNC machining, and Lean/DFA principles — often under real-world manufacturing constraints.
