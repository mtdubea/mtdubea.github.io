About This Project

This project focuses on the development of a custom human-powered racing tricycle built from scratch for the Acadiana 500. Our design emphasizes strength, efficiency, and manufacturability while staying within strict race constraints (no chain, no gear reduction, no suspension).

The vehicle achieved 1st place in competition through exceptional teamwork, testing, TIG welding, and creative mechanical design.
