Profile

I'm a Mechanical Engineering graduate from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette with a strong foundation in CAD/FEA, DFM/DFA, Lean, and root cause analysis. My projects demonstrate hands-on application of engineering design and manufacturing—from precision-machined assemblies to autonomous control systems.

With experience in industrial fieldwork, lab testing, and automation, I focus on delivering practical solutions, system efficiency, and performance optimization. I'm currently pursuing the FE certification and plan to obtain PE licensure while building a portfolio of professional and entrepreneurial engineering projects.

  • Continuous Growth: Committed to refining skills in analysis, Python, and automation.

Goals
