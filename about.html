About Me

I’m Matthew Dubea, a Mechanical Engineering graduate from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. I specialize in mechanical design, manufacturing, quality, and automation. I’ve built award-winning systems and structures across CAD/FEA, welding, machining, and system integration. My work merges hands-on build experience with analytical modeling and process optimization.

From race-ready trike designs to mechatronic payloads, I’m driven by efficiency, function, and long-term reliability. I approach every build with a Lean mindset and a commitment to DFM, FMEA, and real-world tolerancing. Currently pursuing FE/PE licensing and expanding my toolkit with Python and digital workflow automation.
